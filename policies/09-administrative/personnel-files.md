# Personnel Files

MKC Restaurants maintains a personnel file for each employee.

## Your Right to Access

Under Minnesota law, you have the right to review your personnel file. To request access:

- Contact any manager to arrange a time
- Access will be provided within **7 working days** of your request
- You may have a representative present during your review
- You may request copies of documents in your file

## Disputing Contents

If you disagree with any information in your personnel file, you may:

- Submit a **written statement of disagreement**, which will be included in your file alongside the disputed item
- Request that incorrect information be corrected or removed

## Questions

If you have questions about your personnel file, contact any manager.
