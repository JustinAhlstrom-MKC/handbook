# Smoking Policy

MKC Restaurants is committed to providing a safe, healthy, and smoke and tobacco-free work environment for all guests and employees. MKC Restaurants prohibits smoking, electronic cigarettes (including vaping), and the use of any form of tobacco in all indoor space occupied by guests and/or employees, even during non-work time. There is a designated smoking area for those employees who smoke, use electronic cigarettes, or otherwise use tobacco or nicotine products. All use is restricted to this location.
