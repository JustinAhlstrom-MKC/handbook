# Food Safety

The safety of our guests and the quality of our food are top priorities at MKC Restaurants. Every team member plays an important role in maintaining our food safety standards.

## Our Commitment

We are committed to:

- Serving safe, high-quality food to every guest
- Complying with all Minnesota Department of Health regulations and local licensing requirements
- Maintaining clean, sanitary workspaces
- Properly training all team members on food safety practices

## Your Responsibilities

All team members are expected to:

- Complete required food safety training
- Follow proper handwashing procedures
- Handle, store, and prepare food according to established guidelines
- Keep workstations and equipment clean and sanitized
- Be aware of allergen concerns and communicate them appropriately

## Reporting Concerns

**If you see a potential food safety issue, report it to a manager immediately.**

This includes:

- Equipment malfunctions (refrigeration, cooking equipment, etc.)
- Concerns about food quality or freshness
- Sanitation issues
- Pest sightings
- Any situation that could compromise guest safety

You will never be penalized for raising food safety concerns in good faith. When in doubt, ask a manager.

## Questions

If you have questions about food safety practices, talk to your manager or refer to your location's food safety training materials.
