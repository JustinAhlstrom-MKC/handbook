# Personnel Files

MKC Restaurants keeps personnel files for each employee. You have access to those files and may ask that items contained in the files be changed and/or you may add more information to those files if you disagree with the contents. You may view your personnel file by contacting any manager who will help arrange it. There may be remedies available to you if we do not allow you to access your file. For more information, please contact any manager.
