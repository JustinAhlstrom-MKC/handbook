# Minnesota Paid Leave (Effective January 1, 2026)

Beginning January 1, 2026, Minnesota's new Paid Leave law will take effect. This program provides job protections and partial wage replacement to employees who take leave for qualifying reasons, including up to 12 weeks of medical leave and up to 12 weeks of family leave within a 12-month period (combined maximum of 20 weeks). Additional information will be provided as this law takes effect.
