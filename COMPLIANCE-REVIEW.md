## CRITICAL - Fix Before Distribution

These issues create immediate legal exposure.

### Compensation & Wage Issues

- [ ] **Complete Tip Policy** - `policies/04-compensation/tips.md`
  - Current state: Placeholder language only
  - Required: Document actual tip sharing practices
  - Must specify: Who participates, percentages/methods, that managers never participate
  - Risk: FLSA violations, DOL investigation, back pay liability

- [ ] **Fix Meal Break Policy** - `policies/04-compensation/meal-rest-breaks.md`
  - Current state: Placeholder for paid/unpaid status
  - Required: Specify breaks are unpaid, 30 minutes, must clock out
  - Add: "If unable to take full break, notify manager - that time will be paid"
  - Risk: MN wage and hour violation

- [ ] **Fix Overtime Comp Time Language** - `policies/04-compensation/work-hours-overtime.md`
  - Current state: Line 29 implies same-week comp time is okay
  - Required: Clarify that once OT is worked, it must be paid at 1.5x
  - Change to: "You may request schedule adjustments within the same workweek to avoid working over 40 hours. However, once overtime is worked, it must be paid - it cannot be replaced with future time off."
  - Risk: FLSA violation

### Employee Rights Issues

- [ ] **Fix Confidentiality/Wage Discussion Conflict** - `policies/03-conduct/confidentiality.md`
  - Current state: Line 21 prohibits discussing wages with guests
  - Problem: Contradicts Wage Discussions policy; may violate NLRA Section 7
  - Change to: "Respect employee privacy - do not share other employees' personal information with guests without their consent"
  - Risk: NLRB unfair labor practice charge

- [ ] **Add Vehicle Exception to Weapons Policy** - `policies/03-conduct/workplace-violence.md`
  - Current state: Blanket prohibition on weapons
  - Required by MN law: Permit holders may store firearms in locked vehicles
  - Add: "Employees with valid carry permits may store firearms in their locked personal vehicles in accordance with Minnesota law."
  - Risk: Violation of MN ยง 624.714

- [ ] **Add ESST Notice Language** - `policies/04-compensation/pto-policy.md`

  - Add section: "This policy serves as notice of your rights under Minnesota's Earned Sick and Safe Time law. You have the right to accrue and use sick and safe time as described. MKC Restaurants will not retaliate against you for requesting or using sick and safe time."

- [ ] **Add Backup Harassment Reporting Channel** - `policies/03-conduct/harassment.md`
