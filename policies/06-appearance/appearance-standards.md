# Staff Appearance Standards

## Servers

- All black base - long pants, professional
- Clean & pressed apron
- Black non-slip shoes & socks
- No leggings or workout gear

## Hosts

- Business Casual/Professional
- Closed-toe shoes
- Appropriate for "church with Grandma"

## Service Support & Food Runners

- Staff T-Shirt (provided)
- Long black pants, black socks, non-slip black shoes
- No sweatpants or leggings

## All Staff

- Long hair tied back
- Nails clean & trimmed short
- No strong fragrances

## Uniforms

**Front of House:** Apron provided at first shift. Brunch t-shirts provided as needed.

**Back of House:** Shirts provided for up to five shifts each week.
