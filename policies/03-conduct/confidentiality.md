# Confidentiality

During your employment, you may have access to confidential information about MKC Restaurants, our guests, vendors, and fellow employees. Protecting this information is essential to our business and required as a condition of employment.

## What is Confidential

Confidential information includes, but is not limited to:

- **Recipes and food preparation methods**
- **Financial information** (sales figures, costs, pricing strategies)
- **Guest information** (names, contact details, payment information, preferences)
- **Employee information** (personal details, wages, schedules, performance)
- **Vendor and supplier information** (contracts, pricing, contacts)
- **Business strategies and plans**
- **Security codes, passwords, and access credentials**

## Your Responsibilities

- Do not share confidential information with anyone outside of MKC Restaurants
- Only access confidential information necessary for your job duties
- Do not photograph or copy recipes, prep sheets, or other proprietary materials
- Protect guest payment information and never write down full card numbers
- Do not discuss employee wages, schedules, or personal matters with guests
- Keep security codes and passwords private

## Social Media

Do not post confidential business information on social media. This includes sales numbers, internal communications, unreleased menu items, or behind-the-scenes content without management approval.

## After Employment

Your obligation to protect confidential information continues after your employment ends. You may not take or use confidential information for any purpose after leaving MKC Restaurants.

## Questions

If you're unsure whether information is confidential or how to handle a situation, ask your manager.
