# Retaliation

MKC Restaurants will not retaliate against any applicant or employee for reporting, objecting to, or assisting with an investigation related to these policies or federal or state law. Retaliation could take on many forms, including intimidation, threats, isolation, demotion, or termination. If you believe this is happening to any applicant or employee, please contact any manager.
