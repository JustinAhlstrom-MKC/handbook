# Changes to This Handbook

Because the general business atmosphere of MKC Restaurants and conditions are always changing, the contents of this Handbook may be changed at any time at the discretion of MKC Restaurants. No changes in any benefit, policy, or rule will be made without due consideration of the mutual advantages, disadvantages, benefits, and responsibilities such changes will have on you as an employee.
